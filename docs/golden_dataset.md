# Golden Dataset

The 10 question/answer pairs used to benchmark Makena. Each was drawn from official, public Montclair State University policy pages. Contacts shown are publicly listed MSU directory entries.

---

**Q1 — How do I register for classes?**
Register through NEST (https://nest.montclair.edu): log in with your NetID (new students may use CWID + PIN), open the Registration area, clear any holds and confirm your registration time, find classes via "Course and Registration Schedules," then "Register for Classes / Add or Drop," enter CRNs, and submit. If you have mandatory advising, meet your advisor first for a registration PIN. Problems → Office of the Registrar, registrar@mail.montclair.edu / 973-655-4376.
Source: https://www.montclair.edu/red-hawk-central/registrar/registration/registration-faqs

**Q2 — How do I book an advising appointment?**
Through Navigate: https://montclair.campus.eab.com/capabilities#/as3/appointment-new

**Q3 — Am I ready to graduate?**
I can explain the requirements but can't confirm your personal readiness without your record. A bachelor's needs ≥120 semester hours, a minimum 2.000 GPA in both major and overall, and all program/university requirements. Check progress and apply in NEST (students may apply once they've earned ≥90 hours).
Source: https://www.montclair.edu/red-hawk-central/registrar/grades

**Q4 — What is a free elective, and where's the list?**
A course outside your major requirements that counts toward total graduation credits. At Feliciano, the 18-credit minors are designed to count as free electives.
Source: https://www.montclair.edu/business/academic-programs/#bachelors

**Q5 — Retention GPA for an undergraduate Finance major?**
A 2.0 cumulative GPA. Below 2.0 you may be placed on academic notice/probation — meet your advisor right away.
Source: https://www.montclair.edu/red-hawk-central/registrar/grades

**Q6 — I want INFO 401 but haven't taken INFO 266. How do I get a prerequisite override?**
Contact Dawn Ferrara (ferrarad@montclair.edu / 973-655-7847, Room 492) to request an override for an INFO course; explain your prerequisite equivalency. If approved, you still must register in NEST before the add/drop deadline.

**Q7 — Transfer student, 2.6 GPA — can I apply for the combined BS/MS Accounting program?**
No. Transfer applicants need a 3.0+ GPA; below 3.0 you'd apply to the graduate program separately later. Current students also need 90 overall credits and 18 ACCT credits.
Source: https://www.montclair.edu/combined-programs/programs-of-study/accounting-combined-bs-ms/

**Q8 — Freshman resident with 25 credits — parking permit cost?**
Not eligible — resident undergraduates with 29 credits or less can't register or park a vehicle on campus. Extenuating circumstances can be submitted via the parking exception request form (work purposes are not a valid exception).
Source: https://www.montclair.edu/facilities/our-services/parking-services/parking-information-for-residents/

**Q9 — Just placed on academic probation at 1.8 GPA — options?**
You're on probation/academic notice and need to reach ≥2.0. Options: (1) meet your advisor for a recovery plan, (2) free CAST tutoring, (3) review course load and repeat options, (4) if you get aid, contact Red Hawk Central — aid may be affected separately under SAP. Failing to recover in time can escalate to suspension.
Sources: https://www.montclair.edu/cast/ · https://www.montclair.edu/red-hawk-central/registrar/grades

**Q10 — Family emergency, failing two classes — what happens to my financial aid?**
Aid can be affected if GPA and completion pace fall below Satisfactory Academic Progress (SAP). Now: (1) contact Red Hawk Central / Financial Aid about your aid, (2) talk to your advisor about withdrawal/incomplete/grade options, (3) ask about a SAP appeal or mid-year re-evaluation if applicable.
Contact: redhawkcentral@montclair.edu / 973-655-7600 · https://www.montclair.edu/financial-aid/
